LAMP is a resource made available to you by the Digital Psychiatry Program. LAMP allows you to get resources about mental health and take assessments related to mental health.

ACCESS AND USE

By using the LAMP App you understand and agree to the following:

  • The LAMP App is not designed to be used to address medical emergencies. Please call 911 or your local emergency services in the event of a medical emergency.
  • The LAMP app provides mental health educational information and allows you to access use various screening questionnaires and cognitive tests. This information is not medical advice. LAMP is not intended to diagnose or treat any medical condition. LAMP is not continually updated, and new information may emerge that is not reflected in the app.
