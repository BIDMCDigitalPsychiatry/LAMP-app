“Data” a hɛn nsa ka no, yɛboa anon a yɛkyekyɛ mu ma ɔboa hɛn wɔ dwumadzi ne kankɔ na mpontu mu. Yɛfrɛ dɛm dwuma a yedzi no ‘aggegated data’ Dɛm A’aggregated data’ no boa hɛn ma yeker hɛn dwumadzi no ho asodzi a ɔda hɛn do; yetum hu hɔn a wɔsra hɛn, hɔn wobisa hɛn mboa na mfomdo a binom yɛ. Yɛnfa iyi nto obiara do. Dɛn ndzemba (data) yi yetum dze to hɔ mber pii, ber biara yɛpɛ no, yetum dze dzi dwuma biara a ohia, yɛdze hwehwɛ nsɛm mu so hwɛ dɛ obiara mbu hɛn mbra do ama nkorɔfo enya gyedzi wɔ hɛn dwumadzi mu.
