Math Game

1. This game lasts for 1 minute.

2. First, a number will be displayed on the screen.

3. Subtract 7 from it and enter the answer on the screen.

4. Next, subtract 7 again from the entered number and enter the answer on the screen.

5. Repeat this 5 times.

6. The game ends either when the 1 minute completes or after the subtraction is done 5 times, whichever is earlier.
