Temporal Order

1. This game lasts for 4 minutes will be played 2 times.

2. A grid of 9 cells will be seen.

3. Four images will be shown in the grid in a random order.

4. You need to memorize the position of each image in the grid.

5. When the sequence ends, you need to participate in a simple activity such as, answering a survey.

6. Next, you are required to place the images in the positions they were shown in the grid.
