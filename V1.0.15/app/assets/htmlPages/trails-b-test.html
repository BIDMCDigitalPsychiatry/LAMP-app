trails-b Test

1. Tap 1 to start the test.

2. Drag your finger to connect to A which is also the first letter in the English alphabet.

3. Continue to connect to number 2 and then connect to B which is the second letter in the alphabet.

4. Continue connecting the numbers and the letters in this way.

5. Correct connections will be denoted using Green lines. When an incorrect number or letter is connected, the line will turn Red.

6. You can undo an incorrect connection as soon as you make it.
