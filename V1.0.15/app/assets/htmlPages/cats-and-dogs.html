Cats and Dogs(New)

1. This game lasts for 6 minutes.

2. A set of white boxes will be shown on the screen.

3. Next, the boxes will be moved up and dogs will be shown in the place of some boxes.

4. Then, the boxes will slide down into the previous position.

5. You are required to tap the boxes that had dogs behind them.

6. This will continue for 6 levels and more dogs will be hidden at higher levels.

7. After Level 6, cats will also be mixed with the dogs. You will be required to tap the boxes hiding the dogs only.

8. At later levels, you will be required to tap the boxes hiding the cats only.
