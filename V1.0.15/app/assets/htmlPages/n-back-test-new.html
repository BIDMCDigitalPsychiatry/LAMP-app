n-back Test(New) (1-back Test, 2-back Test, 3-back Test)

1. This game is available in 3 levels:

  • a. 1 Back
  • b. 2 Back
  • c. 3 Back

2. Choose a level.

3. Tap ‘Start Game’.

4. A set of images will be shown consecutively.

5. Lookout for the repetition of images.

6. Tap the Green tick mark when an image is repeated and tap the Red X mark each time a different image is shown.

7. In the 1 Back level, the image will be repeated right after it is shown.

8. In the 2 Back level, 1 different image will be shown between an image and its repetition.

9. In the 3 Back level, 2 different images will be shown between an image and its repetition.
