n-back Test (1-back Test, 2-back Test, 3-back Test)

1. This game is available in 3 levels:

  • 1 Back
  • 2 Back
  • 3 Back

2. Choose a level.

3. Tap ‘Start Game’.

4. A set of letters will be shown consecutively.

5. Lookout for the repetition of letters.

6. Tap the Green tick mark when a letter is repeated and tap the Red X mark each time a different letter is shown.

7. In the 1 Back level, the letter will be repeated right after it is shown.

  • For example, G B D D F K…

In the 2 Back level, 1 different letter will be shown between the letter and its repetition.

  • For example, H E R Y L Y J L …

In the 3 Back level, 2 different letters will be shown between the letter and its repetition.

  • For example, U I P F R P O …
