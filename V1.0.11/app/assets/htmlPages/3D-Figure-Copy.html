3D Figure Copy

1. This game lasts for 1 minute and 30 seconds.

2. An image will be shown to you.

3. On the next screen, tap the ‘Pencil’ icon and use your finger to draw the figure you just saw. To clear anything that you’ve just drawn, tap the ‘Clear’ icon shown beside the Pencil icon.

4. You will be required to draw 4 images in this manner.
